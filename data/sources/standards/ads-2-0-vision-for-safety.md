## Evidence-ready excerpts
- [p.3] AUTOMATED DRIVING SYSTEMS 2.0: A VISION FOR SAFETY i Today, our country is on the verge of one of the most exciting and important innovations in transportation history— the development of Automated Driving Systems (ADSs), commonly referred to as automated or self-driving vehicles. The future of this new technology is so full of promise. It’s a future where vehicles increasingly help drivers avoid crashes.
- [p.20] AUTOMATED DRIVING SYSTEMS 2.0: A VISION FOR SAFETY 14 SECTION 1: VOLUNTARY GUIDANCE 10. Data Recording Learning from crash data is a central component to the safety potential of ADSs. For example, the analysis of a crash involving a single ADS could lead to safety developments and subsequent prevention of that crash scenario in other ADSs. Paramount to this type of learning is proper crash reconstruction.
